Our Mission & Vision

Established in 2005, Niaz Foundations has been providing life-changing education and vocational training through its purpose-built schools in the heart of Pakistan's rural areas.

Our Mission

Dedicated to ensuring that all children, regardless of background or ability, have access to general education, vocational training, and opportunities for success.

Our Vision

Changing attitudes toward children with special needs and breaking down barriers to promote their value and worth in society.

Our Values

We believe in installing fundamental human values: Compassion, Respect, Empathy, and Excellence to guide students through life.

Our Model

Niaz Foundations follows the Pakistan national curriculum with adaptations for children with special needs. Our comprehensive approach includes:

  • Kindergarten, primary and secondary levels
  • In-depth teacher training programs
  • Free school transportation
  • Quality assurance standards
  • Academic year from April to March

Our Impact

Since our establishment, we have been making a significant difference in the lives of children with special needs and their families through:

  • Purpose-built educational facilities
  • Specialized curriculum development
  • Community awareness programs
  • Family support services
  • Financial independence preparation
